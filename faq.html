Frequently Asked Questions

How do I sign up?
Show up to the weekly meetings or sign up for the email thread through the website.
When and where are the meetings held?
Usually on Farrand Field but occasionally in the Rec Center depending on the weather.
Do I need my own board and bags?
No, everything is provided.
Does it cost anything?
There is a small fee for the tournaments but the weekly meetings are completely free.
Can anyone join?
Yes.
Do I need to bring my own partner?
You do not need your own partner.
Is it D1?
It is a D1 cornhole club.
How can I get involved in the club leadership?
Towards the end of the year have a discussion with either the club president or the vice president.
Does the club participate in tournaments?
There will be a spreadsheet signup distributed through the weekly email.
Are you better than CSU?
Of course!
